E-COMMERCE DESIGN

2016, july | SPD

TOOLS:

Ray-ban.com

The brief was to review and re-design ray-ban.com/usa with the goal of maximising user experience and revenues.

We wanted to focus on creating an interactive and a memorable experience for the customers with a consistent visual identity and good storytelling, so as to give personality to the website. We started analyzing the pain points of the website and prioritizing them and then started finding solutions through our design and communication.

HOME PAGE GOALS

> understand the type of the site
> include the main navigation categories
> prominent search field
> shoppable images experience
> invest in bespoke imagery and design
> inspiration with the introduction of the social stream

CLP- SUNGLASSES GOALS

> high-level view of products
> bridge between marketing/social channels and the actual products
> direct access throught visual categories
