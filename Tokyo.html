TYPOGRAPHY

2016, feb | SPD

TOOLS:

Tokyo

When I was asked to create a logotype based on the theme “Sci-fi” my first thought was a digital-alien world. I was inspired by sci-fi movies and I’ve decided to build a typeface that was linked to them.

I’ve used thin lines to convey a sense of elegance and dots to complete the letters to make them looking as they come from a new-digital alphabet. For the final logo I decided to use a black background and to create a light-shade to reinforce the digital aspect and add a little bit of mystery.

Starting from that logo I was asked to built a typographic cover for a digital magazine on iPad. My effort was to maintain the taste of mystery and digital using only typographic elements as decoration. I played with the meaning of the world "revelation" to create a hierarchy in the page.

The final step was to design also the rest of the article.
