ART DIRECTION

2016, july | SPD

TOOLS:

Incontro

Bottega Veneta really wants to know its customers to provide an experience that adapts to their personalities. The idea is to build a personal relationship that grows in depth in time between the brand and its customers. It is a fluid and modular system of milestones that the sales assistant can give to users. In this way every relationship is different and the sales assistant can decide at which point to proceed with deeper personal services.

Every relationship starts with an handshake, the experience starts in boutique with a signature

Keep in touch: the store experience continues in digital by creating a personal website for the client to bring the brand and the customer closer.

Commitment: the customers would be given the “Key” of the shop, that would help recognise them all over the world and treat them with the same importance. When a user would became a top client, would have access to all personal services Bottega Veneta is offering.

WISHLIST
After every visit in the boutique, the sales assistent will summarize it in the WISH LIST section that will be displayed as entry point for the customer.

DEDICATED ASSISTANT
In any moment is available for customers the possibility to talk directly with their personal assistant.

PERSONAL SERVICES
The personal services depends according to the deapth of the relationship between the customer and the brand.
> book an appointment
> personal agenda
> try at home
> mantainance services
> runway show previews

YOUR BOUTIQUE
Lets the customers see all the NEW ARRIVALS exclusively according to their taste and preference. It also provides them with STYLE SUGGESTION and how to match their already existing collection with the new products. The section WHAT YOU BOUGHT will suggest on how to take care of the products they bought and remind them when it's time for service and also offer them to make an appointment or organize a pick up for the service.
