SYMPHONIAM PARK

#makeyourmusic

INTERIOR DESIGN

2015, jul | Politecnico

TOOLS:

Symphoniam Park

The whole project is based on a metaphor between architecture and music.

The main goal is the socialization in the district of San Siro in order to create a network through a common worldwide language: music.
We have thought of installations inspired by natural elements in which sounds are played by people with simple and everyday gestures, such as walking, sitting, opening the arms.

> Devices (Kinect, Arduino, RaspberryPie) hidden in the blue parts of the installations, detect the movement and transform it in music.
> A single person can play single sounds, groups of people can create complex melodies.
