SYMPHONIAM PARK

#makeyourmusic

INTERIOR DESIGN

2015, jul | Politecnico

TOOLS:

Symphoniam Park

The whole project is based on a metaphor between architecture and music.

Sounds are played by people with simple and everyday gestures, such as walking, sitting, opening the arms. The main goal of the project is the socialization in the district of San Siro in order to create a network through a common worldwide language: music.

> installations are inspired by natural elements
> hidden devices detect the movement and transform it in music
> blue painted part means music
> individual sounds (alone) / complex melodies (groups)
