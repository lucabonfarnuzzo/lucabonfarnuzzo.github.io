This is the campaign timeline:

The marathon will launch the OSC app: the core of the campaign. The user has to choose a goal to reach by the end of the Olympics.
Once the target is set, the app will start motivating people to reach their goal. After that, the app will automatically disinstall.

The launch of the OSC app will be supported by several touch points (goal tower, values stickers, walks of fame, the winner spot) placed in different cities.

At the end of the Olympic Games a second marathon will count the progresses of the partecipants and those who will beat their first time-race will receive the #OSC t-shirt.
