WEB DESIGN

2015, jul | SPD

TOOLS:

Mutandae

The goal of this class was to built, in Wordpress, a webzine about design and designers.

The name of the webzine is “Mutandae” which in latin means “something that needs to be changed daily” and, in italian, "underwear”. That's why the home changes color everytime according to the designer interviewed that day.

The drafting ask to a young or an accomplished designer the drawing of a pair of underwear, which will be the first step to understand the designer’s method.
